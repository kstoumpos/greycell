User Management with Stripe is possible from within WordPress with WP Simple Pay Lite Manager.

== Description ==

The plugin is an add-on for WP Simple Pay Lite which allows you to view your user transactions within WordPress.

* [Need a solution that works with any plugin?](https://cloudswipe.net/features/?utm_source=wordpress_org&utm_medium=link&utm_campaign=stripe_checkout)

Instead of visiting your Stripe dashboard to review your transactions, you can do it from your WordPress admin area.

> "This plug-in will show Stripe transaction details such as customer name, purchase amount, date, and more within your WordPress admin area"

###Plugin Requirements###

This plugin requires the WP Simple Pay Lite plugin. [Get Simple Stripe Checkout](https://wordpress.org/plugins/stripe/)


###Available in the Pro version Only###

There is also a Pro version that works with WP Simple Pay Pro and contains the following shortcode support.

* **Integrates with WordPress user accounts** - Allow your users to view their transactions with integrated accounts created in WordPress.
* **Users can edit Subscriptions** - Allow your users to sign in and update their subscriptions.
* **Users can Cancel Subscriptions** - Allow your users to sign in and cancel their subscriptions.
* **Expiration Notices** - Receive notices when your customers credit cards are near the expiration date.
* **Payment Failure Notices** - Receive notices when your customers recurring credit cards charges fail.

== Installation ==

There are three ways to install this plugin.

= 1. Admin Search =
1. NOTE: Make sure that you have the WP Simple Pay Lite installed.
1. In your Admin are, go to menu Plugins > Add.
1. Search for `WP Simple Pay Lite Manager`.
1. Find the plugin that's labeled `WP Simple Pay Lite Manager`.
1. Look for the author name `Kyle Brown` on the plugin.
1. Click to install.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. A new submenu item `Manager` will appear beneath the WP Simple Pay Lite menu.

= 2. Download & Upload =
1. Download the plugin (a zip file) on the right column of this page.
1. In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add.
1. Select the tab "Upload".
1. Upload the .zip file you just downloaded.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. A new submenu item `Manager` will appear beneath the WP Simple Pay Lite menu.

= 3. FTP Upload =
1. Download the plugin (.zip file) on the right column of this page.
1. Unzip the zip file contents.
1. Upload the `wp simple pay manager` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory of your site.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. A new submenu item `Manager` will appear beneath the WP Simple Pay Lite menu.
